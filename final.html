ABOUT

Nyodema Women Empowerment Association (NWEA) is a community based non-profit organisation formed by women to promote development initiatives and financial independence for women. Improving the life of women is critical for sustainable development as they play a major role in our societies. Women remain marginalised in many sectors in SSA and the need to close the gender gap is paramount. Currently there are 20 and growing of such groups across the country affiliated with NWEA. The platform has 5 women representatives in the NWEA executive who serve as a linked between the platform and NWEA.

EMPOWERMENT

Empowering the women for financial independence focuses on granting mini loans to support and boost start-up business. Most women find it difficult or lack the capacity to acquire loans from banks or other financial institutions. As a result, it hinders their ability to scale up small businesses. We therefore aim to support them by giving them loans that only attract administrative cost.

AGRICULTURE

Agriculture focuses on supporting farming and gardening needs. Women play a key role in rearing animals and growing crops. We intend to build the platform that promotes vegetable growing all year round and linking them with relevant donors that support such initiatives.

HEALTH

Health focuses on creating awareness through educating them on both communicable and non-communicable diseases. To assist with information and guidance on reproductive health and child health care including nutrition and early marriage support as needed.
